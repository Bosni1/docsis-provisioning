 17       
 18      Forms play a role similar to the one controllers play in the MVC model. 
 19      They serve as an interface between Record objects (the model), and  
 20      GUI editors (the view). 
 21      Each Form holds a reference to a Table object. 
 22       
 23      The heart of a Form is a list of TkVariables, which are used by the GUI 
 24      as data storage of the editor widgets. Forms trace all changes to the variables 
 25      and propagate them to the Record objects. 
 26       
 27      As far as Forms are concerned the Record objects are dumb. The Form does not register 
 28      any handlers for Record's events, therefore all changes to the "current" record made 
 29      directly to the Record (not through the traced variable interface), may be lost.

 31      instance variables worth mentioning:
